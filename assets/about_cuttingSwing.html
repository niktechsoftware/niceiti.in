Cutting & Sewing Trade

Cutting & Sewing is a craftsman vocational Trade. Trade is all about the cutting and sewing fabric and its related other activities such as putting the clothe on table, marking it according to measurement, cutting, sewing and finally setting ironing board and iron. The duration of trade is one year with two semesters of six months each.
Trade is very innovative and career orienting in nature as it gives many scopes after its successful passing. This trade is mostly undertaken by girls/women but in some cases buys also go for it. After passing it they have best option i.e. self-employment. There are many ITIs offering trade due to its importance and which make them self-dependant.
